EDUCATION

Borbonesa works with schools, universities and cultural organisations to run creative learning opportunities in the form of short courses, workshops, talks and participatory events. We work with learners of all ages, backgrounds and abilities and offer exciting and original ways of engaging with subject areas, museum collections which meet the needs and objectives of learners and partner organisations.

The projects featured below are a sample of the activities we've devised and delivered in recent years, including mobile technology projects, book-making workshops, treasure trails and animation workshops. If you would like to talk to us about running a workshop with your organisation, please get in touch, we'd love to hear from you.
