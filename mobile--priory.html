MOBILE @ PRIORY

MOBILE TECHNOLOGY PROJECT*
PRIORY SCHOOL
2012

Borbonesa worked with Priory School over a six month period  on this award-winning Creative Partnerships legacy project to promote and develop the use of mobile technology as a positive and powerful means to engage and enhance learning.

Ideas were refined on an online forum, and a trade show event was used to showcase the learning and capture data on student mobile devices, which is being used to define school polices.

Significant outcomes included lessons highlighting SEAL learning, the use of iPADs, student mobile devices, QR codes and the internet to create engaging experiences. The lesson plans were shared via a digital cookbook, designed by Borbonesa and made available to all staff via download.

*The project won the Secondary School Category of the 2013 Education Innovation Awards ‘Innovate My School’.
“Your involvement in the project really lifted it to a different level. We couldn’t have done it without you."
David Rogers, Curriculum Leader – Geography
